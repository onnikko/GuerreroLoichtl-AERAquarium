X11 RADAR

X11 RADAR is a high-power satellite developed by AER VULT, offering extensive SAR capabilities with technologies proven in previous missions. Equipped with the reliability of cutting-edge technologies, it has the ability to monitor large numbers of targets with fast recurrence frequencies. With improved single-pass performance, it captures high-resolution images across a 100x100 km² theatrical area.

POWER FULL

POWER FULL is a revolutionary propulsion system designed by AER VULT, powered by seven of the most powerful engines in the world. This system uses two types of advanced fuels: Liquid Oxygen (LOX) and Liquid Natural Gas (LNG). Inspired by Saturn V's F1 engines, the BE-4 engine is completely reusable and generates an astonishing 550,000 lbf (2,450 kN) of thrust at sea level, with deep throttle capability. With cutting-edge technology, POWER FULL sets a new standard in space exploration.
